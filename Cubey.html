Cubey the Cube

Cubey has been dragged into the 2nd dimension, and must escape!

"Yeah, its pretty good! Very challenging because most of the jumps you cant see. The player has to do lots of leaps of faith." - Blue Pin Studio

About

Run and jump though multiple levels, As Cubey Learn about where he is, and his way home.

But...

This isn't your ordinary platformer, there are no enemies!!! But plenty of Mechanics, and obstacles like Top-Down Mode, Blue Spike (They Crash your game), and more!!!

Story Mode

Play in story mode where you play all levels in order!!!

Freeplay

Or Play in Freeplay mode Where you don't have to beat the levels in order!!! You can select a level!!!

Extreme Mode

Risk it all in Extreme Mode and try to beat the game without the ability to reload after death.

Controls

WASD to move, arrows to move in top-down mode. F to Toggle Fullscreen, M to return to Title.
